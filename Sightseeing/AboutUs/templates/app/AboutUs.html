About GOST

Our team, GOST, has been haunting the world of route planning since 2025. Born from a uni project and an unhealthy amount of caffeine, we specialize in helping people get from point A to point B without getting lost—most of the time. We may not have superpowers, but we do have algorithms, questionable sleep schedules, and a shared mission: making navigation so smooth that even your GPS gets jealous.

At GOST, each team member brings a unique talent to the table—some code like machines, some debug like detectives, and some just provide emotional support to the Wi-Fi router. Together, we form the ultimate chaos-powered route-planning squad. Whether it’s optimizing paths or arguing about who broke the build this time, we’re committed to delivering a project that works… preferably before the deadline catches us first.
